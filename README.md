Lead Funnel Analysis – Business Analyst Case Study
📊 Project Overview
This project is a business analytics case study focused on understanding and improving student enrollment through funnel and campaign analysis. Using real-world data, the goal was to uncover bottlenecks in the lead-to-enrollment journey and provide actionable recommendations for higher conversion.

🧰 Tools & Technologies
Python (Pandas, Matplotlib, Seaborn)
Google Colab
Excel / Google Sheets
Data Cleaning & Visualization

📁 Dataset Overview
Merged and cleaned 3 Excel datasets with over 150,000+ lead records
Removed duplicates, standardized date formats, and prepared data for analysis.

🔍 Key Analyses
1. Lead Funnel Breakdown
Tracked the full funnel: Leads → Form Filled → Fee Paid → Enrolled
Identified major drop-offs; only 238 enrollments out of 150K+ leads
📉 Biggest loss at the form fill stage.

2. Top Courses by Enrollment
Most enrolled: B.Tech (37.8%), BBA (23.6%), MBA (16.4%)
🎯 Focused promotional strategy recommended for these courses.

3. Regional Enrollment Trends
High engagement from Southern India (Karnataka, Tamil Nadu)
📍 Northern & Northeastern states underperforming – opportunity for growth.

4. Campaign Effectiveness
Top campaigns: ABC/89/API, ABC/87/API
⚠️ Some campaigns had high leads but poor conversions → Need better targeting.

5. Time-based Lead Activity
Peak months: May to August (aligned with academic cycle)
⏰ Timing campaigns during high-activity windows is crucial.

✅ Recommendations
Implement regular follow-ups (automated emails/WhatsApp reminders)
Double down on high-performing campaigns
Promote in-demand courses through testimonials and career outcome stories
Expand outreach in untapped regions (North & Northeast)
Adopt CRM systems for automation and lead nurturing.
